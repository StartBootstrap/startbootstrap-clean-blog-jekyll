News and Updates
  • We have submitted the extended outline of the report on Conceptualising Living Wages in Bangalore Slums to Department of Computational Sciences, University of Amsterdam. We are working towards publishing the results of the study as a book.
  • We have successfully completed our first project with Institute for Plasma Research (IPR) and presented the deliverables at their annual Board meeting in Gandhinagar. The project was received with immense positive feedback from all the reviewers. Two teams in IPR are keen to work with us, we will be applying to extend the current effort.
  • NextWave: We kicked off a project on safety of women in public spaces in September, 2013. We have a cross-cultural collaboration underway, with interns from MediaLAB, Amsterdam as well as International Institute of Information Technology, Bangalore. We update our work on our blog.
  • Onkar Hoysala presented a paper on the Indian Energy Game at the 44th ISAGA Conference held at KTH, Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm, Sweden.
  • We conducted the Indian Energy Game Session with participants from Takshashila on 18 June, 2013.
  • Bharath conducted the Game Design Workshop at OF GAMES, a gaming residency at Khoj International, New Delhi on 28 and 29 August, 2013.
  • We co-facilitated the Gaming Jam event organised by Swissnex India on 1 September, 2013. The summary of the event is on our blog.
  • We conducted a City Game Session with participants from NextBangalore on 28 September, 2013. Here is the link to what happened at the session: http://vimeo.com/75761383.
  • Dr.ir. L.J. de Vries, M.A from Delft University of Technology, Delft visited FoV from 10 September, 2013 to 20 September 2013. During his visit, he worked on the Electricity Market Simulation Game.
  • Sruthi Krishnan joined FoV as a full time researcher in the month of August, 2013.
  • Mosa-al Husseini and Rufael Negash from Malmo University, Sweden are in IIIT Bangalore on a student exchange programme, they are interning at FoV.
  • Bauke Bakker and Marissa Memelink from MediaLAB Amsterdam are working as interns as part of NextWave project at FoV.
  • We were on CNBC Young Turks: http://youngturks.in.com/index.php?y=1&autono=946504. Shows how challenging it is to talk about a fledgling research group in a world where a start-up equals questions about product launches and IPO. We need to work on our elevator pitch. How do we describe an organization that works on strengthening decision-making and participatory processes using games, visualisations, and simulations?
